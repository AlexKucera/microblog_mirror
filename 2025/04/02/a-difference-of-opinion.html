A Difference of Opinion

Apr 2, 2025

I always find it utterly baffling how people see the world totally different when presented with the same set of facts. (I had a discussion with my wife this morning and we both drew completely different conclusions.) The important thing when encountering that is to stay calm and acknowledge that the other person has a right to their opinion. Not every discussion should devolve into a mud slinging contest. In fact, it‘s best when most don‘t.
