New Obsidian Plugin Released - Freewriting Prompts

Sep 19, 2025

I just released v1.0.0 of my new Obsidian plugin Freewriting Prompts.

Generate AI-powered writing prompts for freewriting sessions using Anthropic’s Claude. Break through writer’s block with creative, thought-provoking prompts delivered exactly when you need them.

Freewriting Prompts gives you two ways to break writers block.

  1. Freewriting Prompts simply gives you a configurable number of prompts and appends them to the current note.
  2. Staggered Freewriting Prompts shows you a configurable number of prompts one after the other with a configurable intervall.

Also you can adjust the way each of these prompts are generated to match your writing style as well as possible.
