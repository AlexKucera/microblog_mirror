Paper Test

Oct 7, 2024

This is a quick test about how the Paper app works. I recently read the Paper review by Erlend on his Havn blog, which lead to me reading the terrific three part series by the Paper developer on how Paper came together. It showed a level of care and detail that I highly appreciate.

So now here I am writing this in Paper and really enjoying the experience. I will compare this to Bear in another post. Both offer the ability to publish to Micro.blog as far as I can tell.

Two paragraphs in and Paper already shines. But it does come with a hefty price tag. Considering I don‘t write professionally, it is a bit of a hard pillow to shallow. Let‘s see how it goes in Bear.
